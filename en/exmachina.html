Ex Machina

Drawing done in chalk of a real size plane, draxing inspiration from a technical drawing depicting Bleriot XI, one of the first planes in the history of aviation. On its board, pilot named Adouard Bague tried to cross the Mediteranean sea and possibly crashed near Corsica. His body was never found.


Realized at the artistic residency UTOPIA

Sound | 3:26"

The visitor is invited to sit down in a deckchair and listen to comforting feminine voice, which tells to kep calm and follow certain instructions. The resulting sound oscillated between a seance of hypnosis and flight safety instructions.
